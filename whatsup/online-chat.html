Chat online to a Counsellor!

You can call or chat online about whatever’s on your mind – no problem is too big or too small – and if you just want to have a chat and a laugh, we’re here to listen. If it’s someone elses problem, that’s cool – we can talk about that too. Whatever you want to say, you don’t need to worrry about being judged or getting into trouble. Because a lot of young people use online chat, there is often a wait time before a counsellor is available. So if you need to talk to someone straight away, phone What’s Up on 0800 942 8787 and speak with a counsellor in person.
